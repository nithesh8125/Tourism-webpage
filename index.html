      London, the capital of the United Kingdom, is a vibrant city known for its rich history, cultural diversity, and iconic landmarks. Founded by the Romans, it has grown into a global center for finance, arts, and politics. The city is home to famous sites like Buckingham Palace, Big Ben, the Tower of London, and the London Eye. With world-class museums such as the British Museum and the National Gallery, as well as the renowned West End theatre district, London is a cultural powerhouse. It boasts a blend of historic architecture and modern skyscrapers, along with beautiful parks like Hyde Park and Regent’s Park. As a global financial hub, the city thrives on innovation and business while offering excellent public transport, including the world’s oldest underground railway system. London’s lively markets, diverse cuisine, and dynamic atmosphere make it one of the most exciting cities in the world to visit and live in.
