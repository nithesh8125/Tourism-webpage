   Here are some important places in London:

          1. Buckingham Palace – The official residence of the British monarch.

          2. Big Ben & Houses of Parliament – Iconic clock tower and government buildings.

          3. Tower of London – A historic fortress and home to the Crown Jewels.

          4. London Eye – A giant Ferris wheel offering stunning city views.

          5. British Museum – A world-famous museum with artifacts from around the globe.

          6. Tower Bridge – A stunning Victorian-era bridge over the Thames.

          7. Trafalgar Square – A central landmark featuring Nelson’s Column.

          8. Westminster Abbey – A historic church and site of royal coronations.

          9. Hyde Park– One of London's largest and most famous parks.

          10.Camden Market – A lively market known for street food and unique shops.
